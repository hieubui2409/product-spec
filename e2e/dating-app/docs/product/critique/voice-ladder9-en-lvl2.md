# Critique: all  ·  level 2  ·  lenses: product, tech, market, craft

> Severity tally: blocker 5 · major 8 · minor 3

## Top 3: fix first

1. **[blocker][market] BRD:42** Fika is rated low threat, but it already sits at #1 on Google Play Vietnam with 750k+ downloads, $1.6M seed, AI matching, and the exact "local, safe, meaningful" story Ghép Đôi Việt wants to tell. **The problem:** the differentiation strategy is built on a false baseline. There is no open lane; Fika owns the narrative with a two-year data head start. **A fix:** upgrade COMP-FIKA threat to high, and add a competitive section naming what Ghép Đôi Việt does that Fika does not. If the answer is national reach for P-PROVINCE, build that into an MVP story instead of burying it as a filter in PRD-MATCH.

2. **[blocker][market] BRD:22** BRD-G3 targets premium break-even in Year 2 with zero price point, zero target conversion, zero ARPU floor. At market-average ARPU (~$8.15/yr) and 5% conversion, 100k MAU yields ~$40k/yr, well short of operating cost. **The problem:** a revenue goal without a price-times-conversion model is a wish. The break-even math likely needs 15-20% conversion (triple Tinder's benchmark), and the spec never confronts the arithmetic. **A fix:** add a unit-economics section to the BRD stating estimated annual operating cost at 100k MAU, the required premium ARPU, and the implied conversion rate. If it does not close at Vietnamese willingness-to-pay, add a second revenue pillar before Year 2.

3. **[blocker][tech+product] PRD-CHAT-E1-S4:18** The "absolutely safe messaging" story has two AC that are unfalsifiable wishes ("conversation must be absolutely safe", "system must fully protect the user"), it carries scope:core-value / moscow:should, and it overlaps the real safety work already owned by PRD-SAFETY. **The problem:** the story can never be accepted, sized, or tested. The build team either ignores it or gold-plates an undefined safety surface, inflating the chat backlog with no measurable gain. **A fix:** delete PRD-CHAT-E1-S4 and fold any real intent into PRD-SAFETY as concrete behaviors (block, report-in-chat, harassment-keyword hold), each with a testable Given-When-Then AC. If PRD-SAFETY already covers it, drop it.

## By lens

### Product
- **[major] PRD-EVENTS-E1-S3:18** A full nationwide concert-ticketing business (line-ups, ticket tiers, 4-per-order holds, QR e-tickets, genre filters) lives inside a dating app, serving no pain or gain in VISION or BRD; the spec even concedes it is solution-first at a severe level (PRD-EVENTS-E1-S3:45). **The problem:** ticketing operations (artist relations, payments, crowd safety) is a separate company. Every hour here is an hour off the 7-day sustained-conversation north-star, and the feature has no buyer in the persona set. **A fix:** cut PRD-EVENTS-E1-S3 entirely, or collapse it to a one-line "later vision" note in PRD-EVENTS with zero AC, so a fully-specced story does not signal half-commitment.
- **[major] PRD-EVENTS:36** PRD-EVENTS is called a "flagship feature" yet carries scope:out / moscow:could / horizon:later (PRD-EVENTS:12-14), and no persona is hiring the product for offline events (P-PROVINCE and P-RETURNEE are the worst-served, per PRD-EVENTS:51-52). **The problem:** "flagship but out-of-scope" tells the build team and investors two opposite stories. Taken literally, it pulls engineering and field-ops budget away from the trust and chat core. **A fix:** pick one framing. Either drop the "flagship" language and keep it an explicit parked experiment, or route it through real opportunity validation (which persona, which pain, which metric beyond the indirect MAU proxy) before it earns the label.
- **[major] PRD-PREMIUM:14** BRD-G3 (premium break-even by Year 2) depends on PRD-PREMIUM, yet the PRD is horizon:later and its riskiest assumption (connection-first users paying for boosts / see-who-liked-you) is named but deferred (PRD-PREMIUM:22-24). **The problem:** if that willingness-to-pay belief is wrong, BRD-G3 is unreachable, but the spec only finds out in Year 2. Discovering broken willingness-to-pay at the latest possible moment is how runway-funded products die. **A fix:** run an early, cheap probe (price test or fake-door on "see who liked you") alongside the core in Year 1, and record a kill/continue threshold. Treat the conversion belief as a riskiest-assumption test, not a Year-2 surprise.
- **[minor] PRD-AIREC:88** PRD-AIREC maps to BRD-G2 (weekly-match-rate) while admitting it is me-too and "almost certainly produces worse recommendations than competitors" until real interaction data exists (PRD-AIREC:43-44); a swipe-optimised model can also pull away from sustained conversation (PRD-AIREC:22). **The problem:** if AIREC gets pulled forward on that BRD-G2 mapping, it ships worse than the distance default and risks optimising for raw matches over the 7-day-chat metric. **A fix:** keep could/later, strip the BRD-G2 impact claim until the core has logged real interaction data, and restate its success criterion as "beats the distance-default on match-to-sustained-chat rate", never match count.

### Tech
- **[blocker] PRD-MATCH-E1-S2:21** One AC requires a statistically significant A/B result (right-swipe-to-match lift >=20%, p<0.05) as a delivery condition on a size:L / moscow:must / horizon:now sprint story. **The problem:** A/B significance needs live traffic and weeks of runtime that engineering does not control, so the story can never close at merge time and blocks downstream recommendation-quality work. **A fix:** split into (1) "Implement recommendation ranking" (keep the already-testable filter/cold-start/p95 AC at lines 18-20, 22) and (2) "Validate via A/B test" at horizon:next, with AC about instrumented split-assignment logging; move the 20%/p<0.05 target to the PRD success metric.
- **[blocker] PRD-PREMIUM-E1-S1:20** The "unlock within 5 seconds of completed transaction" AC assumes a payment can complete, but payment gateway integration is declared Won't this round (PRD-PREMIUM:78). **The problem:** no system in scope can emit "giao dịch hoàn tất", so the story either blocks on an out-of-scope dependency or ships a stub that cannot reflect production, putting the BRD-G3 revenue path at risk untested. **A fix:** add depends_on:[PRD-PAYMENT] to PRD-PREMIUM and mark the story blocked until payment is in scope, or reframe the AC around a testable internal event ("when a successful payment webhook is received, the entitlement record is written and the UI unlocks within 5 seconds"), documenting that it cannot be tested end-to-end yet.
- **[major] PRD-CHAT-E1-S3:18** Both AC use unmeasurable words: "mượt mà" (smooth) and "hiển thị nhanh và không giật lag", with no numeric threshold for frame rate, keyboard response, or render time. **The problem:** QA cannot write a pass/fail test. The story closes by gut feel (hiding regressions) or stalls in review over what counts as smooth. **A fix:** use device-class-anchored numbers, e.g. 50-message scroll holds >=55 fps for 5s on a mid-range Android, and the first 20 messages render within 1.5s on 4G, both automatable via Espresso/XCUITest profiling.
- **[major] PRD-SAFETY:75** The only data-handling NFR for liveness verification is "processed safely, not visible to other users", with no mention of PDPD Decree 13/2023 (biometric/government ID), no data residency, no third-party vendor risk, no audit trail beyond the 24-hour deletion AC. **The problem:** collecting CCCD/passport images and facial biometrics without a legal basis, residency constraint, or vendor vetting is real legal and reputational exposure. The build team will pick whatever liveness vendor is cheapest rather than compliant. **A fix:** add an NFR section covering data residency, a PDPD-compliant vendor constraint, a verifiable deletion log, and explicit informed consent with a data-handling summary before biometric capture.
- **[minor] PRD-EVENTS-E1-S2:18** The "Giữ chỗ" seat-reservation AC decrements the remaining count by 1 with no concurrency constraint, no atomic transaction, no last-seat race handling. **The problem:** a read-then-write pattern double-books the last seat under concurrent load. The AC passes single-user and fails in production (lower urgency since the feature is scope:out / horizon:later). **A fix:** add an AC for two simultaneous holds on a 1-seat event (exactly one confirmation, the other an "event full" error, count never below 0) and an NFR requiring atomic decrement via DB transaction or optimistic locking.

### Craft
- **[major] PRD-AIREC:82-84** "cách xếp mặc định hợp lý" (reasonable default sort) hides the actual fallback logic: distance-only, distance+activity, random, or something else. **The problem:** "hợp lý" is evaluative, not prescriptive, so the builder guesses the sorting rule and risks silent drift from intent. **A fix:** state the rule, e.g. "xếp mặc định theo khoảng cách tăng dần, sau đó theo độ hoạt động giảm dần".
- **[major] PRD-AIREC:38** "có khả năng dẫn tới kết nối phù hợp hơn" uses two unmeasured comparatives with no threshold or definition of "suitable". **The problem:** ranking suitability is never operationalized, so the model's training goal becomes a guess and the feature cannot be verified. **A fix:** define it against the north-star, e.g. "match chuyển thành nhắn tin duy trì >= 7 ngày cao hơn so với xếp theo khoảng cách", or link to a numeric success metric.
- **[major][terminology] VISION:27-28** "kết nối thật sự được bắt đầu và duy trì" is broad, while the north-star at VISION:51-52 is the narrower "nhắn tin qua lại liên tục từ 7 ngày trở lên"; later text mixes the two. **The problem:** with "real connection" defined vaguely then operationalized as 7-day messaging, the team may optimize message volume over relational depth. **A fix:** set one canonical definition early in VISION ("Kết nối thật = cặp đôi duy trì nhắn tin qua lại liên tục từ 7 ngày trở lên trong tháng") and reuse that exact phrasing downstream.
- **[major] PRD-AIREC:16-26** The deferral rationale is one nested-clause wall with three conditions and no quantified exit for "đủ dữ liệu tương tác thật". **The problem:** the PO can never say "we hit the threshold, build it now", so the defer stays vague forever. **A fix:** add a measurable gate, e.g. "GATE TO START: PRD-MATCH + PRD-CHAT deliver 7+ day sustained-conversation data for 20%+ of MAU cohorts for 1 month."
- **[major] PRD-PREMIUM:82-85** The free-tier NFR "Hạn mức miễn phí phải đủ rộng... còn dùng được" leaves "đủ rộng" and "còn dùng được" as adjectives without thresholds. **The problem:** product and engineering will argue forever (50 swipes/day vs 5) and ship misaligned to intent. **A fix:** specify a number, e.g. "free tier minimum 20 swipes/day; Premium removes the limit."
- **[minor] PRD-PREMIUM:50-52** Three distinct persona value props (P-URBAN revenue, P-PROVINCE pool-scarcity, P-RETURNEE time-window) are packed into one run-on paragraph. **The problem:** the dense prose risks the builder missing that P-URBAN is the primary revenue driver and prioritizing the wrong persona. **A fix:** split into three one-sentence bullets, one per persona with its specific motivation.
- **[minor] PRD-EVENTS:41-48** The "Lưu ý lệch trọng tâm" note reads as a PO journal entry, with rhetorical framing ("gold-plating") and sarcasm ("vận hành nghệ sĩ, bán vé, an ninh đám đông") inside the executable spec. **The problem:** the build team sees editorial color instead of a requirement, blurring whether the feature is in scope. **A fix:** move the editorial note to a decision-log entry or team notes; keep only the facts in the spec body ("PRD-EVENTS-E1-S3 is scope:out / moscow:could / horizon:later; concert ticketing is an orthogonal business; revisit after PRD-CHAT validates the north-star").
