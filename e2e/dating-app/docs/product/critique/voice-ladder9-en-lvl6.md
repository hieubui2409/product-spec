1. **[blocker][tech+product+craft] PRD-CHAT-E1-S4:18** "Absolutely safe messaging," whose two AC are "the conversation must be absolutely safe" and "the system must fully protect the user." That is not acceptance criteria, that is a fortune cookie. Three lenses tripped over the same line, so this was not subtle. Honestly, reading it wears me out: you typed an unfalsifiable wish, slapped `scope: core-value, moscow: should` on it, and walked away while a real safety surface (block, report, content filter) already lives in PRD-SAFETY. You wanted the credit for "safety" without writing a single testable thing. **Why it's done for:** no engineer can ever close this story, so it loops forever or gets gold-plated into a duplicate of PRD-SAFETY, and your chat backlog carries a ghost that delivers nothing against any BRD metric. **Just retype it:** delete it, fold any real intent into PRD-SAFETY as named behaviors each with a Given-When-Then AC ("tap Block and report: sender can no longer message, account hits the moderation queue within 10s"), or drop it if PRD-SAFETY already has it.

2. **[blocker][tech] PRD-MATCH-E1-S2:21** One AC makes a multi-week A/B significance result (right-swipe-to-match lift >= 20%, p < 0.05) the done-condition for a `size:L, moscow:must, horizon:now` sprint story. You put statistical significance on the definition-of-done line. The maddening part is you clearly know what a real testable AC looks like, the rest of this story has them, and then you bolted a thing engineering literally cannot control (live traffic, weeks of runtime) onto the merge gate anyway. **Why it's done for:** the story can never be marked done at merge time, so it blocks every downstream story that waits on recommendation quality while you wait for production data that does not exist yet. **Just retype it:** split it. Story one "implement ranking" keeps the testable filter/cold-start/p95 AC; story two "validate via A/B" goes `horizon:next` with AC that the experiment is instrumented, not proven. Move the 20%/p<0.05 target to the PRD success metric where it belongs.

3. **[blocker][market] BRD:42** Fika is rated low threat. Fika is #1 on Google Play Vietnam, 750k+ downloads, $1.6M seed, AI matching, female-safety focus, Vietnamese positioning. Every word Ghép Đôi Việt says about itself, local, safe, meaningful, Fika already said and shipped, with a two-year data head start. You filed your only funded local competitor under "distraction." That is not analysis; that is not wanting to look. **Why it's done for:** the whole differentiation strategy sits on a false baseline. You will not enter an open lane, you will enter a lane Fika already owns, and any ML ranking you build later trails their data by two years. **Just retype it:** upgrade COMP-FIKA to high threat ([source](https://saigoneer.com/saigon-technology/20649-made-in-vietnam-dating-app-fika-secures-$1-6m-in-seed-funding)) and add a competitive section that names what you do that Fika does not. ### Tech
- **[blocker] PRD-PREMIUM-E1-S1:20** "Unlock within 5 seconds of completed transaction" assumes a payment can complete, but the same PRD declares payment gateway integration Won't-this-round (PRD-PREMIUM:78). You wrote an AC triggered by an event nothing in scope can fire. **Why it's done for:** no testable trigger exists, so the story blocks on an explicitly out-of-scope dependency or ships on a stub that never reflects production, and your revenue path (BRD-G3) goes untested from day one. **Just retype it:** add `depends_on: [PRD-PAYMENT]`, mark the story blocked, and reframe the AC against a testable internal event ("server confirms a payment webhook: entitlement record written, UI unlocks within 5s"), documented as un-testable end-to-end until a gateway exists.
- **[major] PRD-CHAT-E1-S3:18** "Smooth" and "displays fast and without lag" as AC. No frame rate, no response time, no render budget. "Smooth" again, with nothing in the sentence to measure. **Why it's done for:** QA cannot write a pass/fail test, so it ships on gut feel and smuggles in regressions, or stalls in review while engineers argue over what "smooth" means. **Just retype it:** device-anchored numbers, e.g. "50-message conversation on a mid-range Android, scroll 5s continuous: frame rate stays >= 55 fps" and "conversation screen loads first 20 messages within 1.5s on 4G." Both automatable with Espresso/XCUITest profiling.
- **[major] PRD-SAFETY:75** You collect CCCD/passport images and facial biometrics, and the only data NFR is "processed safely, not visible to other users." No PDPD Decree 13/2023 frame, no data residency, no liveness-vendor vetting, no deletion audit beyond a 24h AC. **Why it's done for:** harvesting government ID and biometrics with no documented legal basis is regulatory and reputational exposure, and the build team will pick whatever liveness vendor is cheapest, not whatever is compliant. **Just retype it:** add an NFR section covering data residency, a PDPD-compliant vendor constraint, a written deletion log verifiable by compliance, and explicit informed consent before biometric capture.
- **[minor] PRD-EVENTS-E1-S2:18** Seat-reservation AC decrements remaining count by 1 with no concurrency constraint and no race handling for the last seat. **Why it's done for:** a read-then-write implementation double-books the last seat under load; the AC passes single-user and fails in production. **Just retype it:** add the concurrent-last-seat AC ("two users press Giữ chỗ on 1 seat: exactly one confirmation, the other gets 'full', count never below 0") plus an NFR requiring atomic decrement.
