# Cross-Brand Discovery Epic

## Goal

A shopper discovers adjacent opted-in brands via an affinity-ranked feed — without Acme becoming a marketplace.

## Business Context

- PRD requirement: personalized cross-brand discovery feed (exploratory; PRD is deferred).
- BRD goal: BRD-G6 (the year-3 cross-brand-discovery network).

## Success Criteria

Discovery click-through (discovery-ctr) demonstrates fans cross brands, with zero paid placement and full brand
opt-in/opt-out control. _Deferred — no target date; the marketplace-risk question is unresolved._

## Scope

A personalized, affinity-ranked discovery feed across opted-in brands, with brand-level inclusion controls and referral
attribution. No paid placement, no engagement-maximizing ranking — those are explicitly out.

## Risks

- **Marketplace re-commoditization** (impact: high, likelihood: low — open) — the existential risk that discovery
  betrays the no-marketplace promise. Mitigation principle: opt-in only, affinity-ranked, never paid placement.
