# Vision — Acme Shop

## Problem Narrative

Boutique fashion brands sell through marketplaces that take 30%+ and bury them under competitors. Their fans want to support them directly but have no easy path. Acme Shop closes that gap.

## Personas

- **shopper** — typically a returning fan of a small brand; values direct connection and is willing to pay a premium.
- **store-admin** — runs one brand; has 1-3 employees; wants a low-overhead storefront.

## Value Proposition

For boutique brands, this is the only storefront that lets them keep 95% of revenue and message fans directly.

## North-Star Metric

Repeat-purchase rate per brand.

## 1-3 Year Direction

Year 1: 100 brands onboarded, checkout flow live.
Year 2: mobile app + multi-region.
Year 3: marketplace discovery without losing direct-to-fan economics.
