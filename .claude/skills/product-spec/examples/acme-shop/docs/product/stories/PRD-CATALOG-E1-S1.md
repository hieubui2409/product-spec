# Add and publish a product — Story PRD-CATALOG-E1-S1

## User Story

**As a** store-admin, **I want** to add a product and publish it, **so that** fans can find and buy it on our
storefront.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given a store-admin on the catalog, when they add a product with title, price, photos, and variants, then it is saved as a draft and appears in the catalog list.
- Given a draft product, when the store-admin publishes it, then it appears on the live storefront within 60 seconds.
