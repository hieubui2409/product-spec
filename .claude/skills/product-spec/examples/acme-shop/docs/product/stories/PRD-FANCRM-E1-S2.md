# Reply inbox — Story PRD-FANCRM-E1-S2

## User Story

**As a** brand-owner, **I want** fan replies in one inbox I can answer from, **so that** I can have a real conversation
with fans instead of losing replies.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given a fan who replies to a broadcast, when the reply arrives, then it appears threaded under that fan in the brand-owner's inbox.
- Given a brand-owner reading a reply, when they respond from the inbox, then the fan receives the response from the brand's authenticated sending domain.
