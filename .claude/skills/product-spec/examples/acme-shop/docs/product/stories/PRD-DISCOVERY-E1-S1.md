# Personalized discovery feed — Story PRD-DISCOVERY-E1-S1

## User Story

**As a** shopper, **I want** to discover other brands I would love, **so that** I can find more makers worth supporting
— surfaced by genuine fit, not ads.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given a shopper who bought from one brand, when they open the discovery feed, then they see affinity-matched opted-in brands and no paid placements.
- Given a brand that has opted out of discovery, when any discovery feed is generated, then that brand never appears in it.
