We need shoppers to be able to sign up with email or Google. Once signed in
they land on the home feed where they can browse curated brands. They should
be able to follow a brand and get a notification when that brand drops new
inventory. Checkout should be one-page; we don't want a multi-step funnel.
Store admins should be able to upload product photos, set prices, manage
inventory counts, and see basic sales reports. We also want a way for
admins to set up a "drop" — a scheduled inventory release that goes live at
a specific time. Long term we want to do gift cards but not in v1.

Two big business goals: (1) onboard 100 boutique brands in 12 months,
measured by brands-with-active-listings; (2) hit 80% 90-day repeat-purchase
rate among first-time buyers.
