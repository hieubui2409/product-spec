# Vision — Legacy Notes

## Problem Narrative

People lose fleeting thoughts because capture tools are heavy. Legacy Notes makes capture instant.

## Personas

- **writer** — captures short notes on the go; values speed over features.

## Value Proposition

The fastest way to capture and retrieve a short note.

## North-Star Metric

Notes captured per active user per week.

## 1-3 Year Direction

Year 1: rock-solid capture + search. Stay deliberately minimal.
